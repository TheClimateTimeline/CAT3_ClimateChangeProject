UK

2035

- Every country worldwide feeling more extreme effects of climate change.
- Sea level rise is rising at a rapid rate threatening lots of coastal communtities.
- Heat waves are appearing more and more affecting those living in desert regions.
- Coal factories still are being built.
- People are starting to get restless about these effects of climate change as it is impacting their lives more and more clearly.
- Climate change organizations are gaining more popularity.

Companies

2040

- Carbon emissions from companies is continuing to rise due to the huge opportunity costs of being net zero.
- Governments plans to tax companies for their emissions backfires as other countries grow economically.
- Climate change activism groups are gaining rapid popularity and influential worldwide
- Lots of climate change policies are starting to be introduced/reintroduced back to the governments
- Developed and large nations such as UK and America has starting to implement climate change policies
- Temperature in tropical areas is almost unbearable
- Increase in wildfires and floods worldwide

India

2045

- An increasing number of countries promise to go net zero
- Floods are occuring twice a year near the most vulnerable coastal communties
- Climate change policies are being argued upon due to arguments about the funding of these policies

- Heat Waves are occuring around every 2 years near tropical regions
